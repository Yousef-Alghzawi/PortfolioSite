Signature Engagements

Services

Each engagement is structured to deliver publication-ready statistical work that withstands peer review. Whether you need a complete analysis from scratch or an independent audit of existing work, the goal is the same: your statistics should never be the reason for rejection.

RCTs Cohort Studies Cross-Sectional Case-Control

Publication-Grade Statistical Analysis

Complete statistical analysis for original research manuscripts, from study design consultation through final reviewer response. You receive not just results, but a defensible methods narrative ready for submission.

Who This Is For

Clinical researchers and academic physicians preparing original research for peer-reviewed journals (JAMA, Lancet, specialty journals). Ideal if you have collected data and need an experienced biostatistician to run and defend the analysis.

Problem Solved

Eliminates the most common reason for desk rejection and reviewer criticism: weak or inappropriate statistical methodology. Every output is designed to pre-empt statistical reviewer objections.

Deliverables

  • Statistical analysis plan (SAP) aligned with your study design
  • Complete primary and secondary endpoint analyses
  • Publication-ready tables (Table 1, regression tables, subgroup tables)
  • High-resolution figures (Kaplan–Meier curves, forest plots, scatter plots)
  • Methods section text ready for manuscript insertion
  • Reproducible R code with full documentation
  • Post-submission reviewer response support
3–6 weeks typical

Not a fit for: Turnaround under 2 weeks, datasets without IRB clearance, or studies without a clear research question.

Systematic Reviews Meta-Analysis Evidence Synthesis

Systematic Review & Meta-Analysis

Complete evidence synthesis following PRISMA 2020 guidelines. I handle the statistical pooling, heterogeneity assessment, and publication bias analysis—you focus on the clinical interpretation.

Who This Is For

Research teams who have completed their literature search and data extraction and need rigorous statistical pooling, or teams planning a systematic review who want methodological guidance from the start.

Problem Solved

Methodologically defensible synthesis that satisfies demanding reviewers. Handles complex scenarios: rare events, continuous outcomes, proportions, and mixed outcome types.

Deliverables

  • Meta-analysis protocol (PROSPERO-ready if needed)
  • Random-effects analysis with heterogeneity metrics (I², Q, tau²)
  • Publication-quality forest plots and funnel plots
  • Subgroup analyses and meta-regression
  • Sensitivity analyses (leave-one-out, influence diagnostics)
  • Publication bias evaluation (Egger, Begg, trim-and-fill)
  • PRISMA flow diagram and checklist
  • Methods and results text for manuscript
4–8 weeks typical

Not a fit for: Reviews without completed data extraction, narrative reviews, or scoping reviews without quantitative synthesis.

RCTs Grant Applications Protocol Development

Clinical Trial Statistical Strategy

Pre-trial consultation and statistical planning. Get your study design, power calculations, and analysis plan right before you enroll a single patient.

Who This Is For

Investigators in planning phase who need robust sample size calculations, a defensible statistical analysis plan (SAP), and mock tables for grant applications or ethics submissions.

Problem Solved

Prevents underpowered studies, inappropriate endpoint choices, and analysis plans that collapse under reviewer scrutiny. Saves months of wasted data collection.

Deliverables

  • Sample size and power calculations with justification
  • Statistical analysis plan (SAP) per ICH E9 guidelines
  • Primary and secondary endpoint definition strategy
  • Mock tables and figures for grant applications
  • Randomization scheme recommendations
2–4 weeks typical
