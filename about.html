Clinical Research Profile

Yousef Alghzawi

Clinical Biostatistician

I provide statistical analysis and methodological consultation for clinical research teams preparing manuscripts for peer-reviewed publication. My work focuses on ensuring that studies withstand rigorous editorial and statistical review.

My approach prioritizes methodological transparency, reproducible analysis workflows, and strict adherence to established reporting guidelines. Every analysis is designed not just to answer the research question, but to survive the peer-review process.

I work primarily with clinician-researchers, academic medical centers, and research teams who understand that statistical rigor is not optional—it is the foundation of publishable, impactful science.

My publications span interventional cardiology (JACC), hematologic oncology (Blood/ASH), neurology, and cardiovascular surgery—giving me cross-specialty fluency that helps me anticipate the statistical expectations of different editorial boards.
